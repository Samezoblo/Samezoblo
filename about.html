Samezoblo in Georgian language means neighborhood or more literally the place where the courtyards are.

The zine is an unconventional mode of representation of city ecologies.
It provides a foreign perspective on casual city life, its objects, and peculiarities, which became normal for the local eyes.

Samezoblo reminds us, that each urban environment is unique and that uniqueness should be appreciated or preserved.
The zine documents objects, which comprise a great part of everyday culture, but will never be officially preserved as a cultural heritage.

In this regard Samezoblo acts as an informal repository of such objects, which will be telling a story of NOW through years, spanning geographical borders.
